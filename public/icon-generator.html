TradeJournal PWA Icons

The SVG icons are located in the public folder.

For production, convert them to PNG using any SVG to PNG converter:

  • pwa-192x192.svg → pwa-192x192.png
  • pwa-512x512.svg → pwa-512x512.png
  • apple-touch-icon.svg → apple-touch-icon.png (180x180)

Or use online tools like CloudConvert
